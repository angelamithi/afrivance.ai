The Problem We Solve

You're not short on information. You're short on time to read it.

Every sale, every delivery, every stock movement in your shop creates data — but most owners only get to look at it once a month, if at all. By then, the shelf that ran empty last week has already cost you sales, and the slow-moving stock has been quietly tying up your cash for weeks.

Without It

  • You only notice a stockout when a customer asks for something you don't have
  • Slow-moving stock sits on the shelf for weeks, quietly tying up your cash
  • Sales reviews happen once a month — by then it's too late to act
  • Spotting trends means digging through receipts or spreadsheets yourself

With Retail Intelligence

  • You know what's running low before it actually runs out
  • You see what's sitting unsold so you can move it before it's a loss
  • You get a clear, plain-language report every single morning
  • You get a heads-up on what's coming, not just what already happened
How It Works

It answers the three questions every owner asks

No dashboards to learn, no spreadsheets to open. Just answers.

"What happened?"
It looks at yesterday's sales — what sold, what didn't, and which branch did best — and summarises it for you in plain language.
"What's happening now?"
It checks your current stock levels and flags what's about to run out and what's been sitting unsold too long.
"What's coming next?"
Using your past sales patterns, it predicts what's likely to sell well in the days ahead — so you can plan and reorder in time.
