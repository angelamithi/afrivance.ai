Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Follow the steps below to permanently delete your Macros AI account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

Option 1: Delete Within the App

  1. Open the Macros AI app on your device
  2. Go to Settings or Profile
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Read the confirmation message carefully
  5. Tap "Confirm Delete" to complete the process

Your account will be deleted immediately, and you'll be logged out.

Option 2: Email Request

If you're unable to access the app, send an email to:

info@afrivance.ai

Include the following information:

  • Subject: "Macros AI Account Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address or Google account email
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account

We will process your request within 7 business days and send you a confirmation email.

Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, you will lose access to all your data, meal logs, progress tracking, and personalized insights. Please make sure you want to proceed before confirming.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your Macros AI account, the following information will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Account Information: Your Google account credentials, profile name, email address, and profile picture
  • Meal Data: All food logs, photos of meals, and nutrition calculations
  • Progress Tracking: Your macro goals, daily intake history, and progress charts
  • AI Insights: Personalized recommendations, eating pattern analysis, and AI-generated suggestions
  • App Preferences: Your settings, notifications preferences, and customizations
  • Usage Data: App activity logs and feature usage statistics

Data Retention Policy

While most of your data is deleted immediately, some information may be retained for specific purposes:

  • Legal Compliance: Transaction records (if applicable) may be retained for up to 7 years as required by law
  • Anonymized Analytics: Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics may be retained for improving our services
  • Backup Systems: Data in backup systems will be completely removed within 30 days
  • Security Logs: Basic security logs may be kept for fraud prevention and platform security

Your Privacy Matters

We take your privacy seriously. After account deletion, we do not retain any personally identifiable information unless legally required. For more details about how we handle your data, please review our Privacy Policy.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about the deletion process, or if you're experiencing issues with deleting your account, please contact our support team:

info@afrivance.ai

Before You Go...

If you're considering deleting your account due to a specific issue, we'd love to help! Common concerns we can address:

  • Difficulty using certain features
  • Privacy or security concerns
  • Technical problems or bugs
  • Suggestions for improvements

Please reach out to us before deleting—we're here to make your experience better!
