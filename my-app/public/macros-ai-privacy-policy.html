1. What Data We Collect

Camera Access and Images

We collect and process the following data related to camera functionality:

  • Camera Access: We require permission to access your device's camera (android.permission.CAMERA)
  • Food Images: Photos of meals that you capture using the in-app camera
  • Image Metadata: Technical data such as image format (JPG), quality settings, and file size
  • Processed Image Data: Base64-encoded versions of your photos for secure transmission

Personal Information

  • Profile data including age, height, weight, and gender
  • Nutrition goals and dietary preferences
  • Email address (for users who sign in with Google)
  • Device identifiers (for guest users)
  • Location data (country, timezone) for culturally relevant meal suggestions

Usage Data

  • Meal logging data and nutritional information derived from your food photos
  • App usage patterns and preferences
  • Device information and technical data
  • AI Coach conversation history and interactions
  • Meal plan requests and generated recommendations
  • Weight history and progress tracking data

Sensitive Health Information (Optional)

If you choose to use our diabetes management features, we collect:

  • Diabetes Type: Type 1, Type 2, Prediabetes, or Gestational Diabetes
  • Target Blood Glucose Levels: Your personalized glucose targets (typically 80-130 mg/dL fasting, <180 mg/dL post-meal)
  • Insulin-to-Carb Ratios: Units of insulin per 10g of carbohydrates
  • Insulin Sensitivity Factors: mg/dL drop per unit of insulin
  • Glycemic Index Preferences: How strictly you follow low-GI foods
  • HbA1c Targets: Your personalized HbA1c percentage goals

CRITICAL MEDICAL DISCLAIMER

The diabetes management features are for informational and educational purposes ONLY. We do not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals (physicians, endocrinologists, certified diabetes educators) for all medical decisions. Never adjust insulin or medication without consulting your healthcare provider. We are not liable for any health outcomes resulting from use of the diabetes features.

2. Why We Need This Data

Camera Access

We need camera permission for the following reasons:

  • To capture photos of your meals: The core functionality of Macros AI is to analyze food images and provide nutritional information
  • Real-time meal scanning: Allows you to take photos of your food directly within the app
  • Quick and convenient tracking: Eliminates the need to manually input food items

Images and Photos

We need your meal images to:

  • Identify food items: Our AI analyzes photos to recognize different foods in your meal
  • Calculate nutritional values: Estimate calories, protein, carbs, and fats based on visual analysis
  • Provide personalized tracking: Store your meal history for progress monitoring
  • Improve accuracy: Allow you to review and correct food identifications

Location Data

  • Culturally Appropriate Meals: Suggest authentic local foods based on your country
  • Meal Timing: Understand when you eat based on your timezone
  • Note: We only collect country and timezone - NOT precise GPS location or movements

3. AI Nutrition Coach Data Processing

When you interact with the AI Nutrition Coach:

  • Conversation Processing: Your messages are processed by Google Gemini AI to generate personalized nutrition guidance
  • Context Analysis: The AI analyzes your profile data, meal history, and dietary goals to provide relevant responses
  • Location-Based Suggestions: Your country and timezone are used to suggest culturally appropriate meals using authentic local foods
  • Conversation Storage: Chat history is stored to maintain context across sessions
  • Meal Plan Generation: AI creates personalized daily or weekly meal plans based on your dietary restrictions, goals, and location
  • Nutrition Inbox: AI-generated insights are delivered to your inbox and stored with your account

How Location Data Works

What we collect: Country name and timezone only (e.g., "Kenya, Africa/Nairobi")

How we collect it: Automatically detected from your device's timezone settings

When we use it: Only sent to AI during your first conversation message

What we DON'T collect: Precise GPS coordinates, street addresses, or movement tracking

Meal Plan Details

Generated meal plans include:

  • Breakfast, lunch, dinner, and 2 snacks per day
  • Complete macro breakdowns (calories, protein, carbs, fats)
  • Portion sizes and cooking instructions
  • Shopping lists with local ingredient names
  • For diabetic users: Additional diabetes information (see Section 4)

4. Diabetes Management Data Protection

If you choose to use the diabetes management features:

Data Security

  • Encryption: All diabetes-related health data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Control: Diabetes data access is restricted to essential systems only
  • Separate Storage: Health data is stored with additional encryption layers
  • Access Logs: All diabetes data access is logged and monitored

How We Use Diabetes Data

  • Meal Recommendations: Provide personalized meal suggestions with low glycemic index foods
  • Carb Counting: Calculate total carbs, net carbs, and fiber for each meal
  • Glycemic Index Ratings: Provide GI and GL (Glycemic Load) for all food suggestions
  • Insulin Guidance: Calculate recommended insulin doses based on YOUR provided ratios (informational only)
  • Glucose Impact Predictions: Estimate blood sugar rise and peak times
  • Eating Order Advice: Suggest optimal order to eat foods (vegetables → protein → carbs)

What We DON'T Do

  • NO Sharing: We NEVER share diabetes data with third parties except as required by law
  • NO Marketing: Diabetes data is NEVER used for marketing, advertising, or sold to anyone
  • NO Research Without Consent: Only included in anonymized research if you explicitly opt-in

INSULIN DOSING DISCLAIMER

CRITICAL NOTICE: Insulin dose calculations provided by the app are estimates based on the ratios YOU provide. These are for informational purposes ONLY. Never adjust insulin or medication without consulting your healthcare provider. Always verify AI recommendations with glucose testing. Monitor your blood glucose before and 2 hours after meals. Keep a food diary to identify personal glucose triggers. We are not responsible for health outcomes resulting from insulin dosing decisions.

Type-Specific Guidance

The AI Coach provides specialized advice based on your diabetes type:

  • Type 1 Diabetes: Precise carb counting, insulin timing, exercise adjustments
  • Type 2 Diabetes: Weight management, insulin resistance strategies, medication timing
  • Prediabetes: Prevention strategies, lifestyle modifications, weight loss support
  • Gestational Diabetes: Frequent small meals, nutrient focus for baby's development, postpartum monitoring

5. How We Use and Store Your Data

Image Processing

When you capture a meal photo:

  1. Local Processing: The image is processed on your device and converted to Base64 format
  2. Secure Transmission: The processed image is sent to our servers using HTTPS encryption
  3. AI Analysis: Google Gemini AI analyzes the image to identify foods and calculate nutrition
  4. Temporary Caching: Images may be temporarily stored on our servers during analysis (30-60 seconds)
  5. Permanent Storage: Once saved, images are stored in Google Cloud Firestore linked to your account

Data Storage

  • Cloud Storage: All data is stored securely using Google Cloud Firestore with industry-standard encryption
  • Local Caching: Images may be temporarily cached on your device for retry functionality if analysis fails
  • Encryption: All data transmission between your device and our servers uses HTTPS encryption
  • Access Control: Only you and authorized system processes can access your meal data
  • Geographic Distribution: Backups are encrypted and stored in secure, geographically distributed locations

Camera Usage Policy

  • Camera access is only activated when you choose to scan a meal
  • No background access: We never access your camera without your explicit action
  • No gallery access: We do not access your photo gallery or any other images on your device
  • No recording: We only capture still photos, never videos or recordings
  • User control: Camera flash and torch features are disabled by default

Data Retention

  • Meal Images: Retained until you delete them or close your account
  • Temporary Caches: Automatically cleared within 24 hours
  • Conversation History: Retained until manually deleted by you or account closure
  • Meal Plans: Retained for 90 days unless saved permanently by you
  • Diabetes Data: Retained until you delete it or close your account
  • Account Deletion: All data, including health information, is permanently deleted within 30 days
